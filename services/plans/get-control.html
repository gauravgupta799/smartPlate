Our Plan for Your Glucose Management Journey

Every person deserves more than a carb-counting handout. We apply medical nutrition therapy and diagnostic insight to help you manage blood sugar with precision.

  • Micro-Nutrient Testing

    We screen for deficiencies (e.g., Vitamin D, B12, Magnesium) that can affect insulin sensitivity, nerve function, and glucose regulation.

  • Blood Sugar Pattern Analysis

    Whether using finger sticks or CGMs, we analyze your data to identify trends, triggers, and meal responses—then build nutrition adjustments that match.

  • Carbohydrate Strategy Mapping

    We help you understand not just how much carb to eat—but which kind, when, and how to pair it to reduce glycemic spikes and crashes.

  • Hormonal & PCOS Support

    For those with PCOS or gestational diabetes, we create strategies that reduce insulin resistance and support hormonal balance through specific meal timing and macronutrient ratios.
